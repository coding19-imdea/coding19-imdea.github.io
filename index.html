FAQ

Can I suggest a problem?

Of course, use this google form to suggest a new problem. Just make sure that if you copied or edited the problem from a different source, your suggestion complies with the source's copyright regulations.

What time are you going to post the problems and how?

We will post the problem in this webpage every MWF morning at 12:30h CET.

How do I submit my response and when?

When you have your response you can submit it using the google form link in the table above.

Do I have to submit the source code of my solution?

Yes! For educational purposes we will post the source code of the three winning solutions. If you don't post your code you cannot be one of the three winners!

Is there a place to discuss the problems?

Of course, there is the coding19 channel at IMDEA Software Mattermost.
